PERFORMERS

Lucky Color Brown
From Melborune, Lucky Color Brown combines funk grooves and sharp rock riffs to create a unique, electrifying sound. Known for their high-energy live performances, the band has earned a dedicated following across Australia's festival circuit, and is here to battle.
Faster Than Parachutes
Faster than Parachutes is a dynamic pop-punk band that brings energy and power to every performance. Their catchy hooks and relatable lyrics have made them fan favourites in the Australian underground rock scene.
Cooper's Life
Right from Byron Bay, Cooper’s Life delicately paces heartfelt lyrics and mellow acoustic tunes, introspection and serenity to the stage. Inspired by the natural beauty of Byron Bay, their music speaks to the soul and tells stories of life, love, and growth.
DJ Kweenie
DJ Kweenie is a rising star in Australia’s electronic music scene, blending house beats with catchy indie vibes. Her performances are a mix of party anthems and deep grooves, guaranteed to keep you jumping all night.
